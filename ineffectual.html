Ineffectual Me

An original slam poem



I woke up this morning
To the mourning doves
Swarming for there's a chance
It'll be storming.

A storm is a forewarning
As the clouds are adorning
Forlorn thorns. Sing

The songs of my people,
But who are my people?
I took a sleep pill
While my bloodline was read
Must've been a sweet thrill
Knowing some creeps will

Interrogate me,
Swear I hate these arrogant fleas,
Their background is an episode of
Youtube's Brief History.
But brief is not me,
I'm a theif of two culture trees,
Jewish Japanese monolingual
Hater of sushi.

Ineffectual me,
Unmedicated ADHD,
Can't sit still while writing poetry,
Call me Jogging K. Rowling.
